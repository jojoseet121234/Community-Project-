About Refugees

Refugees are individuals who have been forced to leave their home countries due to persecution, conflict, or violence. They seek protection and safety in other countries and are unable or unwilling to return to their home countries due to fear of persecution.

Reasons for Displacement

Refugees can be displaced for various reasons, including:

  • War and armed conflict
  • Political instability
  • Religious or ethnic persecution
  • Natural disasters

Refugee Support and Assistance

Many organizations and governments provide support and assistance to refugees, offering:

  • Shelter and accommodation
  • Food and water
  • Healthcare
  • Educational opportunities
  • Legal services
